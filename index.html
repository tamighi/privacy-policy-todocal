Privacy Policy for Todocal

Data Usage:

Todocal is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect any personal information, and there are no user accounts associated with our application.

The application stores locally on your device a set of data for the purpose of providing a personalized experience within the app.

Data Security:

Your data is securely stored on your device using local storage technologies. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Children Under 13:

Todocal is intended for users of all ages. If you are under 13 years old, please do not use our application or provide any personal information. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, they should contact us at tomi-lys@live.fr, and we will take steps to promptly remove such information from our records.

Your Choices:

As no personal information is collected, there are no account-related actions required. You have full control over your locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy:

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at tomi-lys@live.fr.
